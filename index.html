Transcriber

Local, offline Whisper transcription. iPhone-safe adaptive chunking + overlap.
Multilingual needs a non-.en model. Switch model to a multilingual one (e.g. edit dropdown below) if your audio isn't English.
Accuracy boost: chunks overlap and are stitched together. If iOS kills a chunk, the app retries smaller before giving up.
Drop an audio file here, or click to choose .m4a .mp3 .wav .ogg .webm — decoded locally in your browser
Model runs via transformers.js (WASM/WebGPU) after a one-time download, cached by the browser for offline reuse.
